# Motion

<Lede>
  Three durations, two easings, and one explicit prohibition.
</Lede>

## Durations & easings

- **120ms ease** — background and `border-radius` transitions on rows. Fast enough to
  feel like an instant response to hover.
- **140ms ease-out** — overlay and popover entry (vim-help, command palette). Slightly
  slower than rows, with a settled-in tail.
- **250ms ease** — chrome morph (the topbar's merged-state crossfade as the H1 scrolls
  past). Slow enough to read as a deliberate transition rather than a flicker.

## When not to animate

**Never on `box-shadow`** — except the focus-visible outline. Animating shadow
composites every frame and reads as "the panel is breathing"; the resting shadow is
meant to be invisible after first paint.

Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` everywhere. The theme toggle's animated puck and any
sliding underline collapse to instant transitions when the user has reduced-motion set;
nothing else moves under that media query.
