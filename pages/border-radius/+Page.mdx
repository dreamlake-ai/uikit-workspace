# Border & radius

<Lede>
  Five named radii smallest to largest, plus a single hairline weight that draws every
  structural border on the page.
</Lede>

## Radius scale

Five named roles: 3 chips, 6 inputs, 7 rest rows, 8 cards, 10 panels. `--radius` (10px)
is the canonical panel + selection radius — it also covers selected rows and selection
runs so a clicked row sits at the same corner as the panel that contains it.

<RadiiSpecimen />

## Hairlines & dividers

Every structural border on the page is `1px --faint` — `rgba(0,0,0,.08)` in light,
`rgba(255,255,255,.09)` in dark. The contract is that panels, gridlines, and dividers
share this single hairline weight; "incomplete" or annotated boundaries get the dashed
variant `--faint-dashed` instead. The chip background `--chip-bg` is the same ink-tint
family at lower opacity, used for inline code, tag chips, and `kbd` glyphs.
