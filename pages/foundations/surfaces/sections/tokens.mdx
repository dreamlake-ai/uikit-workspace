## Tokens

Three keys, two themes. Light separates `--bg` from `--panel-bg` (subtle cream-on-cream
lift) and shares `--rail-bg` with `--panel-bg`. Dark collapses panel onto bg and drops the
rail to a deeper recess — the figure/ground inverts.
