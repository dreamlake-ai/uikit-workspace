## The surface contract

1. Light separates `--bg` from `--panel-bg`; the rail shares `--panel-bg`.
2. Dark collapses `--panel-bg` onto `--bg`; panels lift via 1px hairline + shadow, not a
   brighter fill.
3. Dark *drops* the rail to `#2b2b31` — figure/ground inverts; chrome recedes, working
   surface advances.
4. `tokens.css` is for surface chrome only — never `--ok` or `--error`. Keep semantic hex
   inline at use sites so `grep` tells the truth.
