## Levels

Three structural sizes, picked by the `level` prop: `h1` (22 / 27 ·
−0.012em), `h2` (16 / 22), `h3` (13 / 18). All three pair with the same
mono-caps eyebrow — 9.5px / 600 / +0.12em / muted at 70% opacity, with a
6px gap to the title below. The sizes are lifted directly from the §03
Typography source in the design guide; they're the same `.ts-h1` /
`.ts-h2` / `.ts-h3` rules every other heading in the system uses.
