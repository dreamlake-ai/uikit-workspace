## Inline within copy

The `<Kbd>` element is meant to sit inside a normal paragraph. It picks up the
surrounding line-height so chords like <Kbd>⌘</Kbd> <Kbd>K</Kbd> read as part
of the sentence rather than as a separate widget. Keep one key per pill; for
chords, render adjacent pills with a small gap rather than packing the keys
into a single rectangle.

Press <Kbd>⌘</Kbd> <Kbd>K</Kbd> to open the palette, <Kbd>esc</Kbd> to dismiss
it, or <Kbd>/</Kbd> from anywhere on the page to jump straight to search.
