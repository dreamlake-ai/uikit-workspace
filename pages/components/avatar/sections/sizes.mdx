## Sizes

The four sizes ported from the design-guide lab — `20`, `26`, `32`, `48`.
Each is tuned to a specific call site: `20` for compact rows, `26` (the
default) for the sidebar / nav-foot, `32` for primary list leads, `48` for
the profile / settings hero. The mono label inside scales to ~40% of the
box, floored at 8px.
