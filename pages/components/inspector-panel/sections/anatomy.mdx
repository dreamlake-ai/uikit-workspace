## Anatomy

Four parts. The **shell** carries `--panel-bg`, a 1px `--faint` border, and a
10px (`--radius-md`) corner — the same chassis every other panel in the guide
pours into. Above the body, the optional **eyebrow** (mono caps, muted) and
**title** (UI 16, ink) form the head; the body is the children slot. Drop the
head when the panel is purely a surface — `<Panel>` with children only is a
valid use.
