## Anatomy

Four named slots flow into the four grid columns. `rowId` pins to the 56px
leader column at 10.5px / muted / 70% opacity — short enough to never wrap, dim
enough to read as metadata. `label` takes the flex column at 11.5px / ink /
500, ellipsing on overflow. `meta` and `status` fall into the auto columns on
the right at 9.5 and 9 px respectively; `meta` carries +0.04em tracking, and
`status` is uppercase and bucket-tinted.
