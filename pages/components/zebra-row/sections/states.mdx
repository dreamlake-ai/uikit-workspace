## States

Four bands: `rest`, `hover`, `selected`, `focus`. Hover swaps to the cool
selectable band; selected swells the radius from 7 to 10px, paints the accent
fill, and forces every span to the row-selected fg so the bucket-tinted status
text doesn't bleed through. Focus draws a 2px accent outline inset by 2 so the
ring sits inside the row's footprint. The states in the specimen are *forced*
via the `state` prop — for interactive use the consumer wires real `:hover` /
`:focus` / `aria-selected` and lets the descendant selectors fire on their own.
