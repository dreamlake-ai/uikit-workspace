## Anatomy

Three slots, left to right: a leading magnifier glyph, the input itself, and an
optional trailing hint chip. The wrapper is the `<label>` so clicking anywhere
inside the bar focuses the input — the `:focus-within` selector then paints the
accent ring on the wrapper without the input needing its own border.

<SearchBarAnatomySpecimen />

The chrome lives entirely on the wrapper: `--search-bg` fill, radius 6, padding
7px / 10px, transparent border. The `<input>` itself is borderless and
outline-less — its job is just to render the typed glyphs. Pulling the focus
ring up to the wrapper means the magnifier and hint chip stay inside the ring,
which is the standard atom shape across the design-guide.
