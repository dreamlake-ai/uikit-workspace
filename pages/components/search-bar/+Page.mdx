# Search bar — `--search-bg` fill · no border · mono placeholder · ⌘K focus · accent ring on focus · ⌘K hint chip

<Lede>
  A small mono input with the dedicated `--search-bg` fill and no border —
  it sits as a tinted slot in the page rather than a bordered control.
  Leading 13×13 magnifier in muted ink, mono 11.5px placeholder at 65%
