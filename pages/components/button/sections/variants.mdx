## Variants

The design-guide ships two. **Ghost** carries inline / secondary actions — anything where the
button is one of several peers in a row. **Primary** is the single accent-fill CTA per
surface; if a screen has two primaries, demote one of them to ghost.

<ButtonVariants />

The leading slot (`icon` prop) renders before the label at .65 opacity, lighter weight than
the label itself — meant for key-combo glyphs (`⌥⌘K`), not full icons. Keep the label as the
visual anchor; the leading slot is metadata.
