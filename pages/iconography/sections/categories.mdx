## Categories

Six buckets cover every glyph the apps need: **navigation** (arrows, chevrons, panel
toggles), **files & data** (folders, files, databases, hashes), **actions** (add,
delete, edit, copy, confirm, dismiss, more), **layout & views** (list, tree, columns,
grid), **status** (the six semantic states from the palette page), and **domain**
(pipelines, branches, history, tags, merges). Each card below renders the glyph at
24×24 with stroke 1.5 and the kebab-case lucide name underneath.
