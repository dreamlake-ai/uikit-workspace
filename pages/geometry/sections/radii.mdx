## Radii

Five named roles, smallest to largest: 3 chips, 6 inputs, 7 rest rows, 8 cards, 10 panels.
`--radius` (10px) is the canonical panel + selection radius — it also covers selected rows
and selection runs so a clicked row sits at the same corner as the panel that contains it.
