## Shadows

Four shadow tints layered onto the panel surface. Light mode uses ink-tinted black at low
opacity; dark mode uses pure black at higher opacity (the same custom property tracks both
themes). Tint-1 is the resting panel base; tint-2 lifts a popover off the page; tint-3
floats a modal or vim-help overlay. The inset variant marks pressed states and focused
inputs.
